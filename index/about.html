About

From building computers in the technology club in high school to starting the journey of becoming a self-taught web developer, I search for opportunities to educate new user groups of the limitless possibilities of technology. Many technical support roles have allowed me to dig deeper into technical conundrums while maintaining a clear and concise line of communication with the tech neophyte. The marriage of consumer relations and technology allow me to share my love of people with my love of technology. The challenges of my career have built me into a better human being holistically, and continuing on this path and strengthening my skills to their greatest potential is my number one priority.
